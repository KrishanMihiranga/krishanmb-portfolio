# About Me

Hello! My name is Kishan Mihiranga and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet.My interest in web development started back in 2018 when I decided to try editing custom Tumblr themes - turns out hacking thogether a custom reblog button taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at an advertising agency, a start-up, a huge coporation and a student-led design studio. My main focus these days is building accessible,inclusive products and digital experiences at Upstatement for a variety of clients.

Here are few technologies I’ve been working with recently:

  • JavaScript
  • Eleventy
  • Node.js
  • React
  • Vue
  • Wordpress
